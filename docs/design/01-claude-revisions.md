## Specific Design Concerns

### The "Warm Amber/Orange" Color for Hearts
**Problem:** Orange/amber is used for **warnings** in Nordic design systems (SL, BankID, government apps).

**Alternative:** 
- **Soft pink/rose** (actual heart color, warm without alarm)
- **Warm terracotta** (earthy, Swedish summer cottage)
- Keep the "glow" concept, just adjust hue

### The "Gesture Slide" Send Mechanic
**Problem:** Sliding is:
- Hard for arthritic hands
- Error-prone (accidental sends)
- Not standard in Nordic apps

**Alternative:**
- Tap recipient
- Confirm amount (with default suggestions: 25/50/100)
- Add optional message
- Send

Boring? Yes. But **it works** and matches mental models from Swish.
